# Cortisol Arcade V1 Asset Spec

This pass uses original procedural bitmap assets generated in-repo. No third-party source images were imported.

## Art Direction

- Dark premium arcade base, close to black but with visible blue-black depth.
- Neon accents are limited to green, cyan, violet, amber, and danger pink.
- Explorer and market assets use scan-grid/network language rather than decorative illustrations.
- Arena and Pong marquees should read as game-center tiles from a distance.
- Host and Client shells should share the same logo mark and color rules.

## Generated Assets

- `ca-logo-mark.png`: rounded square brand mark for shell/launcher usage.
- `cortisol-client-keyart.png`: Client launcher background/key art.
- `arena-marquee.png`: Arena library/launcher card.
- `pong-marquee.png`: Pong library/launcher card.
- `simnet-grid.png`: Explorer/network density card.
